Can you provide evidence of your English language skills?

To apply for QTS, you’ll need to provide ONE of the following:
  • a university degree that was taught in English
  • a Secure English language Test (SELT) from an approved provider, at level B2, completed within the last 2 years
  • evidence that you were born in, or hold citizenship of, an exempt country
Which countries are exempt?

The following countries are exempt from additional English language proficiency requirements:

  • Antigua and Barbuda
  • Australia
  • The Bahamas
  • Barbados
  • Belize
  • Canada
  • Dominica
  • Grenada
  • Guyana
  • Jamaica
  • Ireland
  • Malta
  • New Zealand
  • St Kitts and Nevis
  • St Lucia
  • St Vincent and the Grenadines
  • Trinidad and Tobago
  • USA
  • England
  • Scotland
  • Northern Ireland
  • Wales
