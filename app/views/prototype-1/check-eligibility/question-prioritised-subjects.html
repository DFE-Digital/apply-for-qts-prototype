Does your teaching qualification or university degree qualify you to teach one of the following subjects?

You must be qualified to teach one of the subjects listed to be eligible for QTS.

You’ll need to upload your certificate, along with a transcript that shows all the modules you studied and the scores you achieved.

  • Maths
  • Science
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • French
  • German
  • Italian
  • Japanese
  • Latin
  • Mandarin
  • Russian
  • Spanish
What we mean by qualified
Qualified means EITHER your teaching qualification specifically shows you can teach one of these subjects, OR at least 50% of your university degree was in one of them.
