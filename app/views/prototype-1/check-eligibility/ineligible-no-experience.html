You are not eligible

You are not eligible to apply to get qualified teacher status (QTS) in England because you have not worked as a teacher for at least a year since you qualified.

The requirement for QTS is for a teacher to have been teaching for a minimum of 1 year, involving classes/groups of at least 4 children, on a full-time basis, in any type of mainstream school.
