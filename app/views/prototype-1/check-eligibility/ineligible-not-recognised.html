You are not eligible

You are not eligible to apply to get qualified teacher status (QTS) in England because you are not already a formally recognised/qualified teacher in your country.

The requirement for QTS is for a teacher to have professional status in their country/territory of qualification i.e. they can teach a class of children in a state school of that country/territory, unrestricted and not subject to any sanctions.
