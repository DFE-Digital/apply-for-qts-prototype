Your qualifications

Teaching qualification

We accept the following teaching qualifications:

  • a bachelor's (undergraduate) degree that includes a teaching qualification, for example a bachelor's of education
  • a teaching qualification that's separate to a bachelor's degree, for example a master's (post graduate) degree, diploma, or certificate in education

If your teaching qualification is separate to your bachelor's degree, it must be equal to a UK 'level 6' or higher. Find out more about qualification levels.

You will also need to submit details of your bachelor's degree later in the application.

You must enter the first qualification you have that allows you to teach

If you have more than one teaching qualification enter the first one you received. For example, if you have a bachelor's and a master's degree, and both allow you to teach, enter the bachelor's here.

You can add more qualifications later.
