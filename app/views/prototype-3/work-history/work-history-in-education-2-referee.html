Who should we contact to verify this role?

If your application is successful, we’ll contact this person before awarding QTS.

It’s important to choose a reliable contact who can provide a reference in English.
Choosing someone to verify this role

Make sure you have an up-to-date email address for the person you choose to verify this role. The reference they provide will be important to your application.

We’ll ask them to confirm that you worked full time at the school for the period you’ve told us about.

We’ll also check that you worked unsupervised in planning, preparing and delivering lessons to at least 4 students aged 5-16 at a time, as well as assessing and reporting on their progress.
