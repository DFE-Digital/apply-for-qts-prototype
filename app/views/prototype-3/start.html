Apply to get qualified teacher status in England

Teacher training in England leads to qualified teacher status (QTS). QTS is a legal requirement to teach in many English schools.

If you trained to teach outside the UK, you can use the service to apply for qualified teacher status (QTS).

To make an application, you will need to upload a PDF or jpeg of your:

  • personal identification document, including proof of any name change
  • degree qualification certificate
  • teacher training qualification certificate
  • teacher training qualification transcript
  • letter of professional standing, if you do not have a registration number

You cannot use this service if you have ever been banned from teaching or working with children, or if you have ever started but not completed teacher training in England.
