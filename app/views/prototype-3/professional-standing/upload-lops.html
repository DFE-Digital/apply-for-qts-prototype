Upload a letter of profesisonal standing (and translation if not in English)

A letter of professional standing will confirm qualification of teacher, level of teaching, teaching subjects and any misconduct or convictions. The letter must be written in English. If it is not written in English, you will need a translation and upload both versions.
Where to get this

{% elseif data['country-recognised'] == "India" %} It is understood that Letters of Professional Standing are not issued in India.
Instead, you must arrange for the head of the last school where you taught in an elementary or secondary school in India to send you a letter of reference. The letter must be current. It must list the dates and length of your employment as a teacher and say that you were never suspended for any disciplinary reason and that your conduct was satisfactory.
The Letter of Professional Standing must be signed by one of the Principal, Director, Headmaster or Headmistress of the school.
