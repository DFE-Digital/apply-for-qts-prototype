You need to provide a written statement from [Your authority]

This is called a [Letter of Professional Standing]

If this document is not written in English, you'll also need to upload a translation.

It must confirm:

  • that you've completed a course of initial teacher training (ITT)
  • that you've successfully completed any period of professional experience comparable to an induction period (if required)
  • the age ranges and subjects you're qualified to teach

This written confirmation must be dated within 3 months of you applying for QTS.
