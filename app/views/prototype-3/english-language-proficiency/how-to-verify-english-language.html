You need to provide proof of your English language proficiency

There are 2 ways you can do this.
This is sometimes called a 'Medium of Instruction' (MOI) and you can request it from the institution where you studied. It must be signed and stamped by them on official headed paper, and it must state that the medium of instruction was English. It must also include your name, degree title and award date.
The test must have been taken within the last 2 years. We cannot accept English language tests from a provider that does not appear on the list.
About approved providers

To apply for QTS you must use an approved provider. We cannot accept English language tests from a provider that does not appear on the list.
