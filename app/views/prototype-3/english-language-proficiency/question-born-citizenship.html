Were you born in or hold citizenship of any of the countries below?

If you were born in, or hold citizenship of any of the following countries, you’re exempt from English language requirements. You’ll need to be able provide a passport or other official form of identification.

  • Antigua and Barbuda
  • Australia
  • The Bahamas
  • Barbados
  • Belize
  • Canada
  • Dominica
  • Grenada
  • Guyana
  • Jamaica
  • Ireland
  • Malta
  • New Zealand
  • St Kitts and Nevis
  • St Lucia
  • St Vincent and the Grenadines
  • Trinidad and Tobago
  • USA
  • England
  • Scotland
  • Northern Ireland
