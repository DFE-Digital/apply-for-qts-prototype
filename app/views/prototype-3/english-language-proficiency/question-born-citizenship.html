Your qualifications

Were you born in or hold citizenship of any of the countries below?

If you were born in, or hold citizenship of any of the following countries, you’re exempt from English language requirements.

You’ll need to provide a passport or other official form of identification. You can do this in the ‘About you’ section of the application form.

  • Australia
  • The Bahamas
  • Barbados
  • Belize
  • Canada
  • Dominica
  • Grenada
  • Guyana
  • Jamaica
  • Ireland
  • Malta
  • New Zealand
  • St Kitts and Nevis
  • St Lucia
  • St Vincent and the Grenadines
  • Trinidad and Tobago
  • USA
  • England
